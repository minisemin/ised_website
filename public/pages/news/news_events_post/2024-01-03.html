Winter Break Office Assistant for UIC Office at International Campus (Songdo)

2024. 01. 03

Example_document.docx (16.95KB)

The UIC office at International Campus is looking for office assistants who can work during the winter break period.

  1. Qualification: Undergraduate students with GPA above 1.75 in previous semester
    • International students on leave of absence are NOT ELIGIBLE to apply due to the immigration policy. However, undergraduate Korean nationals on leave of absence and non-UIC students are eligible for the position.
    • Student currently not serving as other type of TA
    • Double majoring students in their last semester(expected to graduate in Feb. 2024), on military leave of absence, student who's status is 'graduated' or 'completed', student taking part in work-study supported by the Korea Student Aid Foundation, student or Mirae Campus(Wonju), Students of Medice/Dentistry/Nursing are limited to apply
  2. Appointment Period: December 22nd(Fri) ~ February 29th(Thu), 2024
  3. Hours: Mon~ Fri 9:00 ~ 17:00, maximum 14 hours/week (Please indicate your availability in the attached document and there must be NO conflict with the class schedule regardless of its structure -virtual / in-person )
  4. Work Location: UIC office at 진리관B (Veritas Hall B), International Campus
  5. Payment: KRW 9,620/hour

Requirements:

  1. Fluency in both Korean and English is a must
  2. Experience working in an office environment is preferred
  3. Student who is accountable on the job(especially coming in on time and keeping the set work schedule with minimum change)

Application Deadline: December 10th(Sun), 2023

Application Process: Fill out the attached application form and submit it to uic_students@yonsei.ac.kr


* If you expect to graduate in February 2024, please let us know in advance

* The application form must be submitted as "[UIC Office TA_Winter 2023] name_student ID number" with the email titled as Fall 2023 UIC Office TA Application (Name)

*There is a possibility of having a brief interview(TBA)


Please feel free to contact uic_students@yonsei.ac.kr if you have any questions.
